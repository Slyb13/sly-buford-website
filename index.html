About Sly

American Investor That Buys & Builds Small Businesses

Sly Buford is an investor, author, and entrepreneur serving as Chairman of Tenth Street Group — a private investment firm focused on buying and building small businesses, the economic engine of America.

Applying Buffett and Munger value investing principles to the small business market, Sly has built a track record acquiring companies across manufacturing, distribution, services, and essential industries.
