Book One

The Hidden Upside: Unlocking Wealth in Small Business Investing

Small businesses are the foundation of the American economy — and the most overlooked asset class in the investment world. In The Hidden Upside, investor and entrepreneur Sly Buford reveals why acquiring and investing in small businesses can offer superior returns, greater control, and a direct impact on real people and communities.

This book is about the opportunity that exists in investing in small businesses. It covers the $10 trillion dollar opportunity of retiring Baby Boomers, how they own a majority of small businesses in America, how small businesses are the economic cornerstone of America, and a host of other topics written for accredited investors ready to explore a new asset class.

Inside the Book

Why small businesses are the most overlooked asset class for accredited investors
The $10 trillion Baby Boomer business transfer opportunity
How to evaluate and acquire small businesses using value investing principles
Why essential service businesses outperform during economic downturns
How to think like an operator-investor, not just a passive capital allocator
