Biography

Official Bio

Short Bio (75 words)

Sly Buford is an investor, author, and entrepreneur, and the Chairman of Tenth Street Group — a private investment firm that buys and invests in small businesses, the economic engine of America. He is the author of The Hidden Upside: Unlocking Wealth in Small Business Investing and applies Buffett and Munger value investing principles to small business acquisitions. Based in Boca Raton, FL.

Long Bio

Sly Buford is an investor, author and entrepreneur and serves as the Chairman of Tenth Street Group, a private investment firm that buys and invests in small businesses, the economic engine of America. Sly has positioned himself as one of the leading voices and minds on business, finance and investing. He is a prolific small cap private equity investor with investments across manufacturing, paper and packaging, distribution and services, and is a bestselling author of The Hidden Upside: Unlocking Wealth in Small Business Investing.
