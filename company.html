About the Firm

Built to Acquire & Operate

Tenth Street Group is a holding company headquartered in Boca Raton, FL, that acquires and operates essential U.S. small and medium-size businesses. We focus on businesses in manufacturing, distribution, services, and infrastructure — industries that form the backbone of the American economy.

We apply value investing principles — buy good businesses at fair prices, operate them well, hold long-term — to the small business market. Tenth Street Group includes multiple operating companies and invests on behalf of accredited investors seeking cash-flowing, real-asset returns.

Investment Thesis

How We Think About Investing

01
Essential Businesses Only

We target manufacturing, industrial, and essential service companies — businesses people need regardless of economic cycles.

02
Value-Driven Acquisitions

We buy at fair prices based on fundamentals — cash flow, margins, management, and moat — not hype or market momentum.

03
Baby Boomer Opportunity

Millions of Boomer-owned businesses are transferring ownership. We see a once-in-a-generation opportunity to step in and continue their legacy.
